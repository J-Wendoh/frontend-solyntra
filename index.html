Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bitcoin Lightning USSD

What is Bitcoin Lightning USSD?

Bitcoin Lightning USSD is a revolutionary service that allows you to send, receive, and manage Bitcoin using simple USSD codes (*384*3036#) from any mobile phone. No smartphone or internet connection required - it works on basic feature phones too. Our MeTTa AI assistant provides intelligent guidance throughout your Bitcoin journey.

How do I get started?

Simply dial *384*3036# from any mobile phone to access the Bitcoin Lightning menu. You can start by buying Bitcoin using your local mobile money service like M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, or Airtel Money. The system will guide you through each step with clear instructions and AI assistance.

Is it safe and secure?

Absolutely! Your Bitcoin is secured by multiple layers: Lightning Network cryptography, our MeTTa reasoning engine, and secure mobile money protocols. Your private keys are protected and never exposed. All transactions are encrypted and verified through the blockchain network.

What's the minimum amount I can buy?

You can start with as little as 10 shs (shillings). This low minimum makes Bitcoin accessible to everyone across Africa and perfect for learning how cryptocurrency works without any significant financial risk.

How fast are transactions?

Lightning fast! Transactions complete in under 3 seconds thanks to the Bitcoin Lightning Network. Once you confirm a payment, your Bitcoin balance is updated immediately, and you can send or receive payments right away. This is much faster than traditional banking or even regular Bitcoin transactions.

Can I withdraw my Bitcoin to M-Pesa?

Yes! You can easily convert your Bitcoin back to your local currency and withdraw to M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Orange Money, or other supported mobile money services. You can also use your Bitcoin balance to buy airtime, pay bills, or top up mobile money directly through the USSD menu.

What languages are supported?

The service supports major African languages including English, Swahili, French, Arabic, Hausa, Yoruba, Amharic, Portuguese, and many others. Our MeTTa AI can understand and respond in your preferred language. You can switch between languages in the USSD menu settings or just start typing in your language.

Do I need internet or a smartphone?

Not at all! That's the revolutionary aspect of USSD technology. It works on any mobile phone - smartphones, feature phones, or even the most basic Nokia phones from 15 years ago. No internet connection, mobile apps, or data plan required. Just dial *384*3036# and start using Bitcoin immediately.

What is the MeTTa AI assistant and how does it help?

MeTTa is our advanced AI reasoning engine that acts as your personal Bitcoin advisor through USSD. It can explain complex Bitcoin concepts in simple terms, help you understand exchange rates, guide you through transactions, provide financial advice, and answer questions in natural language. Just type questions like "What is Bitcoin?", "Show me current rates", or "How do I send money to Nigeria?" in the USSD menu and get intelligent responses.

Which African countries and mobile networks are supported?

We support major mobile networks across Africa including Safaricom (Kenya), MTN (Nigeria, Ghana, Uganda, Rwanda, South Africa), Airtel Africa (15+ countries), Orange (West/Central Africa), Vodacom (South Africa, Tanzania, DRC), Tigo, and many others. Currently active in Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, South Africa, Senegal, Ivory Coast, and rapidly expanding. Check with your local mobile operator for USSD availability.

What are the transaction fees?

Our fees are transparent and competitive: Lightning Network transactions cost less than 1% (often just a few cents), mobile money conversion fees are 1-3% depending on your country and operator, and USSD access is free on most networks. For example, sending $10 worth of Bitcoin typically costs under $0.10 total. All fees are clearly shown before you confirm any transaction - no hidden charges ever.

How do I check my Bitcoin balance?

Just dial *384*3036# and your current Bitcoin balance appears immediately on the main menu screen in both satoshis (sats) and your local currency equivalent. You can check your balance 24/7 from anywhere, even in remote areas without internet. Transaction history, recent payments, and spending analytics are also available through the menu options.

Can I send Bitcoin to someone who doesn't use this service?

Absolutely! You can send Bitcoin to any Lightning Network wallet (like Phoenix, Breez, Wallet of Satoshi) or regular Bitcoin address worldwide. The recipient doesn't need to use our USSD service. You can also generate Lightning invoices to receive payments from any Lightning-compatible wallet globally. The service is fully interoperable with the entire Bitcoin ecosystem.

What happens if I lose my phone or SIM card?

Don't panic! Your Bitcoin is safely secured by our MeTTa system and can be recovered. Contact customer support immediately with your phone number and identity verification documents. We recommend setting up a recovery PIN and backup phone number through the USSD menu. For larger amounts, consider backing up your wallet to a hardware device or writing down your recovery phrase.

How does the AI assistant understand my local language?

Our MeTTa AI system uses advanced natural language processing trained specifically on African languages and dialects. It understands context, slang, and cultural nuances in English, Swahili, French, Arabic, Hausa, Yoruba, Amharic, Wolof, and many others. You can mix languages in the same conversation, and the AI will respond appropriately. Language preferences can be set in the USSD menu.

Is there a daily transaction limit?

Daily limits vary by country due to local regulations: typically $100-500 USD equivalent for basic accounts, up to $2,000+ for verified accounts with ID documents. For example, in Kenya it's 70,000 KES, in Nigeria it's ‚Ç¶200,000. Higher limits available with additional verification. Your current limits are always shown in the USSD menu. The Lightning Network itself has no limits - restrictions come from local financial regulations.

Can I use this for business payments?

Perfect for business! Many small businesses across Africa use our service to accept Bitcoin payments from customers, pay suppliers internationally, send employee remittances, or manage cash flow. You can generate payment QR codes, track all transactions, send bulk payments, and get detailed reports. Business accounts get higher limits, priority support, and integration options with POS systems.
