Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bitcoin Lightning USSD

What is Bitcoin Lightning USSD?

Bitcoin Lightning USSD is a revolutionary service that allows you to send, receive, and manage Bitcoin using simple USSD codes (*384*3036#) from any mobile phone. No smartphone or internet connection required - it works on basic feature phones too.

How do I get started?

Simply dial *384*3036# from any mobile phone to access the Bitcoin Lightning menu. You can start by buying Bitcoin using your local mobile money service. The system will guide you through each step.

Is it safe and secure?

Yes, your Bitcoin is secured by the Lightning Network's cryptography and our MeTTa reasoning engine. Mobile money transactions use secure protocols for additional security. Your private keys are protected and never exposed.

What's the minimum amount I can buy?

You can start with small amounts in your local currency (equivalent to a few cents USD). This low minimum makes Bitcoin accessible to everyone across Africa and perfect for learning how cryptocurrency works.

How fast are transactions?

Transactions are instant thanks to the Bitcoin Lightning Network. Once you confirm a payment, your Bitcoin balance is updated immediately, and you can send or receive payments right away.

Can I withdraw my Bitcoin to M-Pesa?

Yes! You can easily convert your Bitcoin back to your local currency and withdraw to your mobile money account. You can also use your Bitcoin balance to buy airtime or top up mobile money directly through the USSD menu.

What languages are supported?

The service is available in multiple African languages including English, Swahili, French, Arabic, and others, making it accessible to all Africans. You can switch between languages in the USSD menu settings.

Do I need internet or a smartphone?

No! That's the beauty of USSD technology. It works on any mobile phone - smartphones, feature phones, or basic phones. No internet connection or mobile apps required. Just dial the code and start using Bitcoin.
